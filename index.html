Privacy Policy

My Stats does not collect any data of any kind besides that required by a phone to operate apps. All habit tracking data is stored locally on your device only.


User Privacy Policy

No data is collected, used, or shared. Your habits, tasks, and statistics remain completely private and stored only on your device. Users can export or backup their data as needed through iOS features.


Data Storage

All task entries, statistics, and preferences are stored locally on your iPhone using secure iOS data storage mechanisms. This data is included in your device backups but is never transmitted to external servers.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new task?

Tap the "+" button on the Daily Log screen or go to the Tasks tab and create a new task with a custom name and color.

Can I edit past entries?

Yes! Navigate to any previous date in the Archive section and you can add or modify entries.

What do the different colors mean?

Colors are fully customizable categories that help you organize different types of tasks at a glance.

How are streaks calculated?

Streaks count consecutive days where you've logged at least one entry for that task. They reset if you miss a day.
